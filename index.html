Events

AKAD from 16:00 PM

An 'ijab kabul' is the Islamic marriage contract ceremony, which is a fundamental part of a Muslim wedding. It involves the formalization of the marriage in the presence of a guardian (wali) for the bride, witnesses, and a religious officiant. During the 'ijab kabul', the groom makes a solemn declaration of his intention to marry, and the marriage is finalized with the bride's consent, the recitation of prayers, and the signing of the marriage contract. It is considered a sacred and legally binding act in Islam.

VENUE - Biosphäre Potsdam

The lush greenery of over 20,000 magnificent tropical plants, a year-round temperature of 23 to 28°C, a waterfall, and two ponds, along with the intense and mysterious sounds of the rainforest, create the perfect "jungle atmosphere." Terrariums with insects and reptiles, bird aviaries, and tropical fish in an underwater world designed like a historic submarine represent the animal life of the tropics.
Guests at our wedding are welcome to explore the 7,000-square-meter Biosphere Potsdam during their visit (until 19:00 PM). Tickets are already included with your invitation.

Arrival by Public Transport

From Potsdam Main Station

Take line 96 towards Campus Jungfernsee and get off at the "Volkspark" stop, right in front of the Biosphäre Potsdam. Alternatively, take tram line 92 towards Bornstedt/Kirschallee, get off at the "Campus Fachhochschule" stop, and walk 300 meters along the tracks turning to the right to reach the Biosphäre Potsdam.

From Berlin

The Biosphäre Potsdam is easily accessible from Berlin via public transportation, thanks to trains and S-Bahn connections. From Berlin, you can take the S7 line or several regional trains (e.g. RE1), all of which stop at Potsdam Main Station.

Arrival by Car

If you're arriving via the motorway, take the following exits:
From the north: Berliner Ring A10, Exit 25 Potsdam Nord.
From the south: Berliner Ring A10, Exit 17 Potsdam Süd, then B2 via Michendorf.
From the southwest: Berliner Ring A10/115, Interchange 16/9 Nuthetal, Exit 6 Babelsberg.
From the east: Coming from Berlin, Interchange 4 Zehlendorf.
The traffic guidance system in Potsdam will direct drivers straight to the Biosphere Potsdam. In Potsdam's city center, please follow the signs for "Biosphäre" or "BUGA-Park."
There are 160 parking spaces for cars (including accessible parking spaces) as well as bus parking located directly to the right of the Biosphäre Potsdam.
